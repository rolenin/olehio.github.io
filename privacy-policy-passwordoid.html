Information collected by the Passwordoid application

The Passwordoid application does not collect or share any user data.
This means that the user data is never transmitted off the user's device either to the developer or to a third party.
The app developer may collect information you provide directly when you contact them for support or feedback. This may include your name, email address, and any information you choose to share.
If you have any questions, please contact the developer by rolenin.tester@gmail.com

Information collected by AdMob

When you use the Passwordoid application, the AdMob ad network may collect certain information for the purposes of ad personalization and analytics. This information may include:

  • Device Information: such as device type, operating system, and unique device identifiers.
  • User Location: if you have granted the app permission to access your location.
  • Usage Information: including app usage, ad engagement, and other interactions with the app and ads.
  • Cookies and Advertising IDs: AdMob and its partners may use cookies, advertising identifiers, and similar technologies to collect information about your device's interactions with ads.
